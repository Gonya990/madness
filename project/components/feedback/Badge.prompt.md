A compact pill for status, counts, and categories.

```jsx
<Badge variant="success" dot>Running</Badge>
<Badge variant="warning">Building</Badge>
<Badge variant="primary" leftIcon="git-branch">main</Badge>
<Badge variant="danger" solid>3 errors</Badge>
```

- Soft tint by default; `solid` for a filled high-contrast pill.
- `dot` adds a leading status dot; `leftIcon` takes a Lucide name.
